Ella





Ella is a small mountain town in the badulla district of uva province, Sri Lanka, about 1000 meters above sea level. This area rich in bio-diversity with numerous varieties of flora and fauna. Ella is surrounded by hills covered with forests and tea plantations. The town has a cooler climate than surrounding lawlands. Town of ella is approximately 200 kilometres east of Colombo. It located on the Colombo-Badulla railway line, and the A16 highwaya a part of the Colombo-Badulla road.
