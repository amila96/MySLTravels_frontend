Description :

Sri Lanka is one of the few places in the world that can offer such an extraordinary combination of diversity, cultural heritage, people, wild life, adventure, and outdoor luxury experiences. This magical island was once known as Serandib, Taprobane, the Pearl of the Indian Ocean, and Ceylon. Go in search of herds of elephants, Sri Lankan sloth bears and elusive leopards.
